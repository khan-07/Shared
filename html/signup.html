What is BookSpace?

BookSpace is an online book sharing platform focused towards people who love books and want to share them with the world. Book space gives you the chance to showcast your books to the people by making your own "Bookshelf" which other people can see — of course if you want them to. Moreover, you can also make any of your book from the bookshelf up for grabs, that means people can either buy your book or you can lend your book to them. It also provides you the opportunity to discover Bookshelfs which other people have prepared.
